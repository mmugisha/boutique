Victoria View

Hotel / Tour / Travel

Discover Story

Our Hotel

Each room in our boutique hotel has beautiful views of Lake Victoria. In the evening, you can enjoy gorgeous sunsets and take in the cool lake breeze as you sip your drink on the large balconies attached to every room. Our friendly staff will prepare for you delicious breakfast with all-natural ingredients every morning during your stay.

Touring in Uganda

Our experienced team can tailor-made a tour of Uganda's beautiful scenery and wildlife just for you. We organize tours to see the mountain gorillas at Bwindi Impenetrable National Park. You can also see the tree-climbing lions, giraffes, rhinos, cheetahs and other animals at the expansive Queen Elizabeth National Park. You can also see the powerful Nile River or visit the thunderous Murchison's Fall in North-West Uganda.

HOTEL AMENITIES

Breathtaking Views, Delicious Food & Comfy Rooms.

Rooms

All rooms in the estate have a gorgeous view of Lake Victoria. The lake breeze brings in clean, fresh air that you can enjoy on the room balconies. The bed mattresses are comfortable enabling you to have a restful night and wake up feeling refreshed in the morning.

GYM & Wi-Fi

Fast internet is available via Wi-Fi is from every room in the hotel. The gym located on the first floor has treadmills, cycling machines and weights for your morning work-outs. The Estate is located in an area with a number of hills with steep running tracks that will challenge your stamina.

AIRPORT PICKUP & CAR HIRE

We can arrange for our professional drivers to pick you up from Entebbe International Airport on arrival. We have comfortable vans that can be hired for large parties. We also have well-maintained 4x4 cars that you can hire for your own use while in Kampala.

Neighborhood

The Estate is located about ten minutes from Lake Victoria Serena Hotel which has a beautiful 18-hole golf course along the Lake Victoria shores. The City of Kampala is a 15-30 minutes drive depending on traffic.

Directions

The hotel shuttle can pick you up on arrival at Entebbe International Airport.

From Entebbe

The drive from Entebbe International Airport to Victoria View Estate will take about half an hour. The Estate is on Kigo road off Entebbe Road. Kigo Road will be on your right and is located between Kajjansi and Lweza, towns that the taxi drivers are familiar with. It's also the road that leads to Lake Victoria Serena Hotel. See the map below for exact directions and please call our hotel number with any questions.

From Kampala

The Estate is on Kigo road off Entebbe Road. If you're coming from Kampala, Kigo road will be just after the 'Fresh Cuts' meat factory. After the factory, you'll make a left immediately after FuelX gas station. Kigo road also leads to Lake Victoria Serena Hotel, a landmark the taxi drivers are familiar with.
